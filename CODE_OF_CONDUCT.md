# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming and respectful community.

Everyone is welcome regardless of experience level, nationality, or background.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Be helpful.
- Welcome newcomers.
- Accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on improving the project.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Spam
- Discrimination

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
